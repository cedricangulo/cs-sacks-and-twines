## Quick Defense Notes

- `Yes` rows are safe to present as implemented.
- `Caveat` rows are still implemented, but the repo uses a slightly different or broader architecture than the rubric wording assumes.
- The only items that cannot be proven from the repo alone are server-only settings such as phpMyAdmin credentials and php.ini hardening.
